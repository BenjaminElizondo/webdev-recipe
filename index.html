CHICKEN AND BROCCOLI BOWTIES



Ingredients

  • 12 oz pasta
  • 1 lb frozen broccoli florets
  • 3 Tbsp butter
  • 3 Tbsp grated Parmesan
  • Salt to taste
  • Cracked pepper to taste
  • Pinch crushed red pepper

Steps

  1. Bring a pot of water to a boil, then add pasta. Boil the pasta for 7-10 minutes. Add frozen broccoli florets to the boiling pasta water, turn off the heat, and let sit for 1-2 minutes, or until the broccoli is tender-crisp. Drain the pasta and broccoli in a colander.
  2. Transfer the pasta and broccoli back to the pot or to a bowl and add the butter. Toss until the butter has melted and coated everything. Add the Parmesan, salt, freshly cracked pepper, and red pepper flakes, then toss to coat again. Taste and adjust seasoning as needed. Serve immediately.
